United States Green Party: Fundraising Data Analysis



It All Began When...

S. Sutton & Associates Inc., a network of consultants that help nonprofit organizations and philanthropists maximize their philanthropic efforts, was enagaged by the United States Green Party (GPUS) in 2018 to create a 'Strategic Multi-Year Development Plan & Implementation Strategy' to increase their fundraising efforts.

One of the deliverables was to develop a comprehensive fundraising plan with strategies to increase GPUS’s fundraising efforts from individuals, major donors, and special events.

I provided analysis and metrics on their existing donor base to identify opportunities for GPUS to grow its major donor prospects and convert single-gift donors to monthly donors, especially leading into the 2020 presidential election.

Analysis

GPUS uses the NationBuilder fundraising CRM platform with a PostGreSQL database which I queried to create datasets and upload to Power BI.

Captured 2018 and 2019 donation metrics to update GPUS’ rolling 5 year fundraising Key Performance Indicators.

Mined the entire donor database (2002 - 2019) to capture additional donation metrics and trends, created Major Gift and Planned Giving donor prospect lists, donor segments for marketing campaigns, and interactive dashboard for GPUS to explore yearly metrics across various donor groups and donation amounts.



Results


S. Sutton & Associates Inc. provided a robust SWOT analysis, fund development plan, 3-year budget, and Case for Support.

Marketing services included a communications plan, copywriting, social media content development and lead generation from donor analysees.

The national party raised a record donation amount of $463,084 for the 2020 election, and several states raised record donation amounts.

S. Sutton & Associates Inc. secured the Green Party of Canada as a client based on the work completed for the US party.
